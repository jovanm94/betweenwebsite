Between provides a platform for easy invoice financing for small and medium enterprises.
We bring suppliers, buyers and investors in one place and facilitate the factoring process.
Our platform supports both direct and reverse factoring process.

Mission

Challenge

Optimal and timely financing for a business is difficult to obtain. Many factors such as days receivable, days payable, exchange rate risk, planned and actual sales and others contribute to overall financing terms which can result in inadequate liquidity position for a company.
Banks can however encounter difficulty in locating the source of risk in factoring request. All the analysis and necessary steps in each request can result in inefficient and expensive process for the bank.

Solution

We have built a platform that bridges these gaps. Through a user-friendly interface, companies can request financing in a matter of minutes - whether for all their invoices, specific ones, or a specific amount.
Companies can track their financing behavior, use convenient metrics tailored to their business and efficiently monitor their liquidity position.
Banks can request invoice verification for selected debtors, utilize preliminary risk profile for each company and implement their own risk metrics. This way, bank can streamline their approval procedure, make it faster and as a result process more requests along with lots of useful metrics.
